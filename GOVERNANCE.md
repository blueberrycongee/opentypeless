# Governance

OpenTypeless is currently maintainer-led.

## Decision model

Until the maintainer group expands, major product and architecture decisions are made by the core maintainers.
Contributors are encouraged to propose changes through issues, discussions, and pull requests.

## How decisions should be made

We prefer decisions that improve:

- desktop product clarity
- correctness and maintainability
- privacy transparency
- contributor friendliness
- long-term architecture fit

## What requires discussion first

Open an issue or discussion before making large changes to:

- product scope
- desktop architecture direction
- storage formats with migration impact
- privacy and data handling policy
- licensing or governance changes

## Maintainer expectations

Maintainers should:

- explain major decisions clearly
- keep the roadmap current enough to be useful
- review contributions in good faith
- avoid surprise scope changes when possible
