# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want OpenTypeless to be a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful and professional
- assume good intent, but discuss impact clearly
- give actionable technical feedback
- focus on the work, not the person

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or discrimination
- personal attacks
- repeated bad-faith arguments
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when needed to protect the community.
